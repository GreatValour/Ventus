Process Payments Securely, Efficiently, and with Fewer Fees

Safe and effective payment processing is critical to any company accepting credit or debit cards from customers — and these days, that's just about everyone. Use Ventus Pay's end-to-end payment processing capabilities and put yourself at a competitive advantage.

  • Accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, JCB, UnionPay, and ACH payments
  • Benefit from one of the lowest credit card processing rates in the industry for small and medium-sized businesses
  • Take advantage of end-to-end processing: no middleman, fewer fees, more efficient problem-solving when a merchant needs support
  • End the struggle to find reliable and affordable financial services for businesses considered high-risk, including CBD
  • Gain peace of mind from our advanced security supported by proprietary tokenization services and hosted payment center for your customers to self-pay
  • Your single source for payment gateway, processing and acceptance solutions including DIY webstore builder, shopping cart, hosted payment page, terminals
  • Securely store payment data for returning customers

Create a Seamless Payments Experience with
Hosted Payments

Hosted Payments helps your customers feel more secure with a branded payment form on your website when paying for goods and services.

  • Quick and easy setup—customize colors and add your logo
  • Reduce abandonment with a streamlined, familiar checkout and payment experience
  • Convenient for customers to set up recurring payments for memberships, subscriptions, or other ongoing purchases
  • Check out as a guest or create an account for faster future payments or donations
  • Gain peace of mind knowing you've removed sensitive card data from your environment, which reduces the cost and effort associated with PCI compliance

Use Ventus Pay Invoice and Get Paid Faster

Bring your invoicing process into the modern age! Our Ventus Pay Invoice tool makes it easier for your clients and a lot better for you. Learn more!

  • Send e-invoices that include a Pay-Now button
  • Reduce your days sales outstanding (DSO) by giving customers easier ways to pay
  • Reduce the time and errors of handling paper checks and manual input of payments
  • Customize invoices to reflect your company brand
  • Streamline the payment process — offer subscription and recurring billing options
  • Send payment requests and automated reminders via email and text
  • Better manage your cash flow with our analytics dashboard to see the status of all invoices

For NetSuite users, getting paid just got faster! You can bring your invoicing process to the modern age with Ventus Pay's Link-to-Pay for NetSuite.

Mobile Card Reader with Virtual Terminal Lets You Accept Payments In the Office and On the Go

At a trade show or an event? Out on service calls? No Problem!

Turn any mobile device into a payment terminal with Ventus Pay's Mobile Acceptance by SwipeSimple. The mobile app makes it easy to set up shop wherever you are–quickly and efficiently.

  • Easy to set up and intuitive to use, key, or swipe options, including chip cards
  • Easily store card information to better serve your loyal customers
  • Accept tips or create custom preset tip amounts
  • Send receipts via email or text
  • Set up custom tax rates for business across municipalities
  • Utilizes the bluetooth connection on your mobile device

Make Any Computer a Virtual Terminal

Need to take a payment in the office, over the phone, or through the mail? No worries. Easily use any Internet-connected device to accept payments with our Virtual Terminal.

  • Use your computer, tablet, or smartphone
  • Flexible and accessible, all you need is an Internet connection
  • Securely store customer payment card data for future purchases
  • Get started quickly with no hardware investment required
  • Optional hardware to accept EMV transactions

Terminals to Make In-Person Payments a Breeze

Our modern and smart POS terminals are secure, compliant, and reliable. Reduce your risk of fraud while cutting your cost to accept any credit or debit card payment.

  • Choose the right terminals for your business from industry-leading providers
  • Painlessly remove roadblocks to getting paid with a range of devices: EMV capable (chip cards), wired, wireless, and mobile options
  • Maximize your payment acceptance options with check processing and gift cards
  • Increase your data security by storing transaction data on the host server rather than on your POS terminals, with our Host Capture feature
