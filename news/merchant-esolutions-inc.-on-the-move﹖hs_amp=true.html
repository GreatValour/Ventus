Ventus Pay Inc. On the Move

Written by MerchantE | May 5, 2020 11:52:00 AM

ATLANTA--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Merchant e-Solutions completed the move of their headquarters today. The move doubles their office space in the heart of metro Atlanta's fintech hub, Alpharetta, Ga.

“This move facilitates our growth plan and includes top-tier amenities for our employees, such as an onsite restaurant, gym and nature trails,” shares Mike Rouse, CEO of Merchant e-Solutions. “We look forward to being part of the Alpharetta community.”
