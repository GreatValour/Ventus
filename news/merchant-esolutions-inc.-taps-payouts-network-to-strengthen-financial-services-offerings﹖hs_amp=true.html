Ventus Pay Inc. Taps Payouts Network to Strengthen Financial Services Offerings

Written by MerchantE | May 19, 2020 9:02:46 PM

Payment processing platform deepens position as the premier solution for growing businesses.

Merchant e-Solutions, an end-to-end digital commerce platform located in Alpharetta, GA, announces a strategic partnership with Payouts Network, an industry-leading, intelligent enabler of real-time payments. This partnership with Payouts Network will enable Merchant e-Solutions to go to market quickly with new funding opportunities for businesses—while facilitating even more business capabilities in the future. This partnership is the first of several strategic alliances that Merchant e-Solutions has planned for 2020 to expand their product and service offerings for growing businesses.

“Developing the right strategic business alliances is a crucial part of our vision for future growth and success. Partnering with an industry innovator like Payouts Network, presents Merchant e-Solutions with the capability to provide our clients with multiple options to help them grow their businesses faster, and more securely,“ said Mike Rouse, Chief Executive Officer, Merchant e-Solutions.

“A company with a solid foundation built on the strength of dedicated leaders with a commitment for excellence, is the perfect partner - that’s Merchant e-Solutions. Their unique end-to-end digital commerce platform is a differentiator and sets them apart. Together, we’ll be able to offer clients the ability to streamline payments and more easily manage their money“, said Keith Smith – Founder and CEO, Payouts Network.

About Payouts Network   
Payouts Network, Inc. is innovating the next generation of payments by enabling businesses to originate, approve and issue payments in real-time to consumers, employees and businesses. Their industry-leading intelligent platform transforms the complexity of payments into an economical, highly secure and fully transparent process that delivers the speed, security and control required by businesses and consumers. Headquartered in Bozeman, MT, Payouts Network enables businesses to streamline their payment processes across multiple industries, including travel, hospitality, insurance, financial institutions, large employers and more. For more information please visit www.payoutsnetwork.com.

Merchant e-Solutions provides a financial technology platform that drives digital commerce and supports the money management needs of growing businesses.
