MerchantE Announces Enhancements to Business Essentials Bundle

Written by MerchantE | Nov 12, 2020 2:00:07 PM

Upgraded Package Expands E-Commerce Services to Fuel Business Growth

ALPHARETTA, Ga. (Nov. 12, 2020) – MerchantE, a leading end-to-end digital commerce platform, announces new features for the Business Essentials bundle. The all-in-one package includes everything a business needs to conduct commerce online, in the office and on the go.

The business tools that were once limited to large enterprises are now available to any business—large or small. MerchantE’s Business Essentials bundle is a suite of services to support businesses online, giving them greater insights to make better-informed decisions through advanced reporting and analytics.

The Business Essentials bundle provides everything needed to:

  • Easily set up and manage an online store and shopping cart.
  • Add a shopping cart to Facebook, Instagram or an existing website.
  • Support online orders with curbside pickup.
  • Accept in-person payments anywhere, turning any mobile device into an EMV payment terminal.
  • Accept payments online for both individual purchases and for recurring monthly payments or donations.
  • Advertise on Facebook, Instagram and Amazon directly from your website dashboard.
  • Support card payment processing with PCI compliance and security built in.

“Now, more than ever, businesses need to be wherever their customers shop. We’ve simplified how growing businesses manage all the moving parts to make that happen,” says Sandra Blair, Chief Product Officer at MerchantE. “MerchantE’s Business Essentials bundle includes all of the capabilities to meet the financial service needs of businesses, enabling them to take control of how money flows into and out of their business.”

MerchantE’s Business Essentials bundle offers a full range of payment acceptance tools for one low monthly fee and includes one of the lowest credit card processing rates in the industry for small and medium-sized businesses.

To learn more and apply, visit MerchantE.com.
