MerchantE Joins NetSuite Now On Air Virtual Conference Lineup

Written by MerchantE | Oct 21, 2020 2:05:18 PM

Leading FinTech Platform to Showcase New Solutions for NetSuite During Webinars and Breakout Session

ALPHARETTA, Ga. (Oct. X, 2020) – MerchantE, a leading end-to-end digital commerce platform, announces participation at NetSuite Now On Air Virtual Conference. This new, FREE virtual event experience is taking place throughout the month of October. Each week will tackle specific themes, including Finance, Commerce and Operations.

As a Gold Sponsor, MerchantE has a virtual booth and will host a breakout session, How to Adapt to the 2020 Paradigm Shift in Commerce and Financial Operations, on October 22 at 2:00 p.m. ET. During the session, MerchantE will showcase tips that NetSuite users may not know about SuitePayments and new tools to digitize financial services that enable customers to self-pay, reduce human intervention in processing payments, and accelerate payouts to a diverse workforce for earned pay and business expense reimbursements.

MerchantE is also hosting a 5-minute demo series that shares quick tips on managing money for growing businesses. The series’ schedule includes:

 

  • Wednesday, October 21, 3:00 p.m. ET- 3 Tips You Probably Don’t Know About NetSuite SuitePayments. Register Here
  • Tuesday, October 27, 1:45 p.m. ET- Stop Tying Up Worker's Cash with Antiquated Expense Reimbursement. Register Here
  • Thursday, November 5, 1:00 p.m. ET- Gain Hiring Advantage by Paying Workers At Any Frequency, Even at the End of Shift! Register Here

This popular 5-minute demo series has struck a chord for NetSuite users who have expressed their appreciation for the opportunity to get a quick hit of information before committing to a more in-depth look at the new solutions. Two previous sessions in this series included the MerchantE Invoice Link that adds a customer self-pay button to NetSuite invoices and quotes, and the Interchange Optimization Savings Program that helps NetSuite businesses save money on payment processing fees for eligible business-to-business transactions.

“We’re excited to be partnering with NetSuite and to be a part of the Now On Air Virtual Conference,” says Sandra Blair, EVP Product, Marketing & ES at MerchantE. “We are making it easy for NetSuite users to access our most ground-breaking solutions that help businesses digitize their financial operations as the market adapts to the new normal.”

Breakout session and webinar attendees will automatically be entered into a drawing for a chance to win Air Pods Pro or $250 Amazon Gift card. To learn more and register, visit the NetSuite Now On Air Virtual Conference registration page.
