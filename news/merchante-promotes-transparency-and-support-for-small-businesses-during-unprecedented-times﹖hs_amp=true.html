MerchantE Promotes Transparency and Support for Small Businesses During Unprecedented Times

Written by MerchantE | Jul 8, 2020 1:58:19 PM

Company Issues Statement to Disavow Practice of Withholding Reserves from Customers; Calls for Industry to Follow

ALPHARETTA, Ga (July 8, 2020) – MerchantE, a leading end-to-end digital commerce platform, reaffirms its commitment to growing businesses in response to recent reports of payment companies withholding money from customers.

Addressing the topic, MerchantE’s CEO, Mike Rouse, states:

“Small businesses are the foundation of the U.S. economy. They account for 99.9 percent of all U.S. businesses and are the most vulnerable during challenging times, such as the global health pandemic we’re all facing today. With limited cash flow and capital, it’s more important than ever for financial partners to support these growing businesses, opposed to taking advantage of them for personal financial gain.

As a payment provider, it’s our singular focus to help businesses manage their money. In policy and practice, we do not regularly or systematically withhold a portion of our clients’ funds. It’s their money. It’s our role to make it as easy and fast for them to access it. We would like to see this pledge from entire industry.”

MerchantE’s Commitment
For more than 20 years, MerchantE has been committed to assisting small businesses manage and grow their money – securely and transparently. In response to COVID-19, the company waived the monthly fees on its most popular services to help customers continue to operate without burden. In addition, it recently launched same-day funding for established clients to allow for quicker access to funds.
