Retail ISO & Agent Partners

Merchant Services for Resellers

Flexible, profitable revenue-sharing models

Retail ISOs, Agents and Merchant Service Providers (MSPs) can enjoy residual payments by partnering with Ventus Pay. Our partner program is built on 20 years of experience in the payments industry and is the go-to tailored solution to meet your specific needs. Ventus Pay can offer you:

  • Fully PCI compliant and secure payment processing
  • Multiple sponsor banks for high or low risk clients
  • Unmatched revenue share
  • Direct connectivity to the card networks
  • Extensive reporting
  • Boarding and support tools

Unmatched value and solutions

Unlike some payment processing companies, our platform is directly connected to the major card brands. We completely remove the need for a middleman and make it easy to satisfy in-person and online payment processing needs for your business. We also enable resellers to facilitate one-time and recurring payments for all Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover payment methods including credit, debit, prepaid, and ACH.

The Ventus Pay platform is built to drive more value to your customers. Whether your client uses an omni-channel approach or has an international branch, our platform will support your needs with products you can sell.

Extensive experience. Unparalleled expertise.

Experience is key to business success. We’ve partnered with various financial institutions to convert hundreds of merchant services portfolios to our network. We provide staff training, regular program reporting and a highly-skilled client support team. 

Enjoy peace of mind knowing you’re partnering with an industry leader that is transparent, secure, and committed to outstanding customer satisfaction.

When you align with Ventus Pay you get a partner you can rely on when questions about any aspect of the payment process arise. We built it, we own it, we run it. If you ever have questions or need help, there’s only one call to make to your assigned account representative.

Plus, we are there for your clients with our award-winning team of US-based Customer Care specialists, available around the clock (24/7) to support your customers’ payment processing and financial services needs.

Contact one of our specialists today to learn more about partnering with Ventus Pay an industry leader that is transparent, secure, and committed to outstanding customer satisfaction.
